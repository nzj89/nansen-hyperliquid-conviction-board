Step 1

Read Top Recommendations Right Now first. That is the compressed answer: what still looks actionable, how late you probably are, how much money is still in, and whether traders are already crowded and green.

Step 2

Check timing before you fall in love with the idea

Use Best Long Entries and Nansen Market Pulse together. If the wallets look great but the market pulse is overheated and the late-risk is high, the board should talk you out of chasing.

Step 3

Only then open the trader and wallet detail

Drill into the trader rows and the detail drawer to see full wallet addresses, current PnL, recent adds, who is already green, who is underwater, and whether the same traders usually win on this exact symbol and side.

About

What This Project Does

Built for one practical goal: help a busy human decide what is worth stalking, what is too late, and what should be ignored.

Value

It narrows the chaos

Instead of drowning you in every Hyperliquid wallet, it filters toward slower, higher-conviction traders and turns them into a board of setups, long ideas, timing reads, and risk warnings.

How It Uses Nansen CLI

It turns raw wallet data into trade context

The refresh script pulls from nansen research perp leaderboard, nansen research profiler perp-trades, nansen research profiler perp-positions, nansen research profiler pnl-summary, and nansen research perp screener, then reconstructs trader behavior: hold windows, same-asset campaign history, daily consistency, downside profile, confluence, clustering, market regime, and current open risk.

Method

It distrusts tiny samples on purpose

Win rates are reconstructed from closed campaigns, not raw fills, and then trust-weighted so a wallet with two lucky trades does not look equal to a wallet with deeper evidence.

Why It Helps

It answers the actual trading questions

Am I early or late? How long could this trade still have? Which longs are still workable? Which setups are crowded? Which traders are worth stalking instead of blindly copying?

What It Is Not

Not blind copytrading

This is a judgment layer, not a one-click signal copier. The point is transformed analytics and better decisions, especially if you have a normal job and only check the market a few times a day.
